Insulin affordability has got a bit easier this year for the Washington residents with diabetes. To support low-income Americans by increasing their affordability for insulin, HOUSE BILL 2662 has imposed a $100 cap on patient copayments throughout 2021.

The law is applicable for any health care plan issued or renewed this year that covers insulin drugs. This law is practically a blessing for the people living with diabetes as this disease is a potential risk factor for COVID-19, and insulin is like life support for them.

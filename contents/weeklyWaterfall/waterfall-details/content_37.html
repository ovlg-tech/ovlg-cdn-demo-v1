The two most popular strategies for paying off your debts include - Debt Snowball and Debt Avalanche. The Debt Snowball method involves paying off your smallest balances first to create momentum and encourage you to keep slaying those debts. The debt snowball plan helps you build confidence and positive repayment habits by prioritizing the "easy" debts first. The Debt Avalanche method involves paying off your balances with the highest interest rates first. This plan prioritizes efficiency and aims to be the indisputably cheapest and fastest way to get out of debt. So, choose the debt plan which suits you the most and get debt free.

Are you looking for low-cost alternatives to your prescribed medications? Here's good news for you!

SB 0537 was recently signed into law by Maryland's governor and came into effect on October 1, 2021. This regulation mandates that pharmacists or their designees, under certain conditions, inform their customers of the available, lower-cost alternatives to the prescription initially provided. Customers must also be informed about the pricing difference between a therapeutically equivalent drug and a specific prescribed drug.

SB 0537 would also compel pharmacists to retain a record or provide notice demonstrating that clients were informed (verbally or in writing) that the mentioned generic product is equivalent to the brand name product at the time of purchase. This law covers not just prescription pharmaceuticals but also medical equipment that are prescribed.

Do you have a pet in your house? Do you love your pet a lot? Even if you do, please don't carry the animal on your lap when you’re driving. You’re at high risk of getting ticketed under distracted driving laws. You can be imposed a heavy fine.

In New Jersey, you can slapped with a fine between $250 and $1000 for improperly transporting an animal. So, next time when you take your pet in your car be extra cautious and save a big amount.

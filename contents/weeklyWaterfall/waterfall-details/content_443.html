Do you belong to a low-income household and need to weatherize your home?

California's Low-Income Weatherization Program (LIWP), administered by the California Dept. of Community Services and Development, will assist such households in weatherizing their home. This program (SB 535) provides eligible low-income households with rooftop solar systems, solar hot water heating systems, and energy efficiency upgrades at zero cost.

Eligibility criteria:
  • Property must be located in a CalEPA Disadvantaged Community.
  • At least one building on site must contain 5 units or more.
  • At least 66% of residents with incomes equal to or less than 80% of the area median income (AMI).
  • Must be able to achieve at least 15% energy saving.

LIWP is designed to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and household energy costs by saving energy and generating clean, renewable power for single households and multi-family affordable housings. Parallelly, it also helps reducing household energy costs for low-income families and contributes to community health by improving air quality.

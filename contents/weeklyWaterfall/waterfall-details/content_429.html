Are you worried about your student loan debt payment? Well, you don't need to be, not until the end of September '2021.

With the pandemic still raging, President Biden requested the Department of Education to extend the pause on federal student loan payments and collections. The Department of Education has decided to do it while keeping the interest rate on loans at 0% till the end of September 2021.

This is undoubtedly great news for the debtors who are facing financial hardship due to the pandemic. Since this is an interest-free period, you can utilize the extra money you save towards your high-priority bills or other high-interest debt payoffs.

Is playing Pokemon causing an excessive data wastage and higher phone bill? You can reduce it to some extent by following a few simple tips and these are:

  1. Watch out for the public places that offer free Wi-Fi. For example, Pokestops and public library.
  2. Turn off push notifications for other apps when you’re playing the reality game.
  3. Turn off background apps.
  4. Download your local city map before trying to catch Pikachu.

You can download your local map data in the following ways:

  • Open Google Maps
  • Tap on settings
  • Tap on “Offline Maps”
  • Tap on the plus sign
  • Select the area you’ll be using frequently
  • The map will install and integrate with the Pokemon Go app

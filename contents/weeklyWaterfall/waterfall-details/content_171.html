Beware of the laws before you take your car on the High-Occupancy Vehicle (HOV) lane, more popularly known as the ‘carpool’ lane since you have to pay $100-$481 for violating the rules. Some of the rules you need to follow are:

  1. You can drive a vehicle having 2 or more occupants in the peak hours (6 a.m - 10 a.m) and (3pm and - 7pm) on the HOV lane on Monday through Friday.
  2. Other vehicles can use the lanes in off-peak hours
  3. In case of a ‘qualified clean alternative fuel vehicle’, one is free to travel in the lane without extra passengers
  4. One can’t cross over double yellow lines to get in or get out of a carpool lane.

    About

    Let’s face it, attorneys don’t have a great reputation, and for good reason. Client communication often lacks and legal services can be shoddy and overpriced. With this in mind, Derek Beutler founded Beutler Law Offices with the goal of redefining the attorney-client experience. Life is hectic, so at Beutler Law, we strive to make the legal process as pain-free as possible. To do this, we use technology to make the paperwork hassle-free and offer evening and weekend appointments. At Beutler Law, we encourage clients to call or text anytime! We work hard to earn and keep clients’ trust. Whether assisting with bankruptcy matters, debt settlement, foreclosure issues, or back taxes, we believe each one of our clients deserve to be treated with kindness, respect, and professionalism.
